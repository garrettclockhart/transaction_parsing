How to Use

  1. Copy transaction data from your bank statement or app

  4. Use "Copy CSV" or "Download CSV" to export your cleaned data

Supported Formats

  • 3-column: Date, Description, Amount
  • 2-column: Date, Description (amount embedded in description)
